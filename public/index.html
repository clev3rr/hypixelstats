Change Log

This page tracks every update made to the site: what changed, why it matters, and when it was released.

April 3, 2026 (23:45) — Capture the Wool + Comparator UI + Arcade improvements

Today we completed major UI and stats alignment across the comparator and main stats pages, with special focus on Capture the Wool, SkyWars Ranked, Arcade modes and Build Battle.

  • SkyWars Ranked section no longer shows empty placeholder text and appears only when real data is available.
  • Capture the Wool now renders as a dedicated module with a complete mode stats table and additional summary metrics for wins/losses/draws and wools.
  • Stability improvements in stats extraction and fallback handling ensure blank/missing values are handled gracefully.
  • SkyWars and Capture layout were adjusted to prevent drifting table columns and keep consistent responsive appearance.
  • Comparator layout was polished to match Player/Guild style, including icon header alignment and cleaner section spacing.
  • Arcade/Build Battle support extended, with better mode grouping and consistency in reporting numeric totals.

April 2, 2026 (23:45) — Player Comparator Expansion & Polish

Major update to the Player Comparator feature: all 21 minigames are now supported with improved typography, smart mode handling, and complete stats coverage.

Comparator: Game Coverage
  • Expanded comparator to include all 21 configured minigames in alphabetical order: Arcade, Arena, Battleground, Bedwars, Duels, GingerBread, HungerGames, Legacy, MCGO, MurderMystery, Paintball, Quake, SkyClash, SkyWars, SpeedUHC, SuperSmash, TNTGames, TrueCombat, UHC, VampireZ, and Walls3.
  • Games with rich mode implementations (Arena, Bedwars, Quake, SkyClash, SkyWars, SpeedUHC) now display both Overall stats and a detailed Modes comparison table, while 15 other games show Overall only for clarity.
  • Added automatic conversion of simple game configs (GAMES_CONFIG_SIMPLE) to comparator format, ensuring VampireZ, GingerBread, TrueCombat, and others display correctly.
Comparator: Visual & Data Refinement
  • Increased typography across comparator section: title (+1px), section labels (+1px), table text (+1px), headers (+1px), player summary text (+1px) for improved readability without distorting layout.
  • Implemented smart section headers for UHC: Overall, Teams, and Solo are now visually grouped with consistent styling.
  • Removed duplicate "Overall" heading in UHC; added visual separation for Teams and Solo sections to reduce redundancy.
  • Filtered out low-value fields: removed "Time in forcefield (HH-MM-SS)" from Paintball comparator since it typically shows 00:00:00.
  • Added fallback logic for TNT Tag Wins to handle both `wins_tnttag` and `wins_tag` keys, improving data retrieval reliability.

April 2, 2026 (20:17) — Major Stats Update

Today, two major features were added: a full Guild Stats experience and a new Player Comparator section. Below is a detailed breakdown of what was implemented.

Guild Stats
  • Added a dedicated guild search flow that supports both player-name lookup and direct guild-name lookup, leading to a standalone guild results page.
  • Built a detailed guild overview card showing tag, creation date, member count, current coins, total EXP, joinability status, and description.
  • Implemented a guild level and structure panel with role breakdown (leaders, officers, members) for quick guild health visibility.
  • Added a full members table with avatar, guild rank, join date, and last login; player names are clickable and open that player’s stats directly.
  • Improved data accuracy for guild level calculation, wins display source, rank formatting and prefix colors, plus correct rank-based member sorting.
Player Comparator
  • Added a dedicated two-player comparison workflow with a separate results page and a clear back-to-search action.
  • Included core network-level comparisons (level, karma, achievement points, quests, total wins/kills) alongside per-mode game comparisons.
  • Expanded mode-level metrics for BedWars and SkyWars with key ratios and combat stats (K/D, W/L, Final K/D, beds, finals, and more).
  • Introduced visual advantage highlighting (better/worse) so stat differences are immediately readable without manual calculation.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the site and how to use the main sections.

Q

What can I do on this website?

A

You can check player statistics, view detailed guild statistics, compare two players, read project updates in Change Log, and use Contact Us to report bugs.

Q

How do I find a guild?

A

Open Guild Stats in the sub-navigation and search by either a player name or a guild name. The results page includes guild info, level, wins, and member list.

Q

How does Player Comparator work?

A

Enter two player names in Player Comparator. The tool shows side-by-side totals and per-mode comparisons with visual highlights for stronger values.

Q

What if I find a bug or wrong value?

A

The site is still fresh and improvements are ongoing. Please report bugs through Contact Us and include as many details as possible about what happened.

Q

Do I need to refresh the page to switch sections?

A

No. Navigation is handled inside the interface, so sections open instantly without full page reloads.

Q

How does API Compliance work on this site?

A

This is an unofficial project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Hypixel. We only request public profile/guild data needed for your search, use short server-side caching to reduce repeated requests, and do not run continuous player session tracking.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use this website, why it is processed, and what choices you have. By using the site, you agree to this policy.

2. Data We May Process

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, request timestamps, and basic server logs required to run and protect the service.
  • Usage data: pages visited and interactions necessary for troubleshooting and improving user experience.
  • Local browser data: theme preference stored in browser storage for essential site functionality.
  • Search input data: usernames and guild names entered for stats lookups are processed to fulfill your request.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide core functionality (player stats, guild stats, comparator, and other site features).
  • To maintain service stability, security, and abuse prevention.
  • To debug errors and improve performance and reliability.

4. Third-Party Sources and Services

This site may request data from third-party game-related APIs/services (for example Hypixel or Mojang related services) to display public statistics. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

4.1 API Compliance

  • Unofficial status: this website is not affiliated with or endorsed by Hypixel.
  • Data scope: only data required to fulfill user-initiated stats requests (such as usernames, guild lookups, and related public profile/guild fields) is requested.
  • Caching: server-side response caching is used to reduce duplicate API calls and lower API load.
  • No continuous tracking: the project does not perform background session tracking or continuous polling of player progression.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

This site does not rely on tracking cookies for advertising. It may use browser storage for essential UX preferences like theme selection.

6. Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold. Data may only be shared when technically required to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, or protect the website against abuse.

7. Data Retention

Technical logs are kept only as long as reasonably necessary for security, diagnostics, and service maintenance, then deleted or rotated according to operational needs.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to access, correction, or deletion of certain data. You can contact us and we will review requests in good faith.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is not intentionally targeted at children under 13, and no knowingly collected personal information from children is intended.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date on this page.

11. Contact

If you have privacy-related questions or requests, please use the Contact Us page and include enough detail so we can help you quickly.
